Born in Penang, Malaysia. Studied Bachelor of Applied Arts at University Malaysia Sarawak. Post-graduate MSc Digital Futures at Institute of Digital Art and Technology, University of Plymouth, United Kingdom. A minimalist designer and a full-stack web developer who has a thing for science, technology, art and philosophy. A wonderer searching for a theory of everything.

**2004 - Present**\
Works as a Web Developer/Designer, Data Analyst, either as a freelancer, a contractor, a collaborator, or as an employee, for various individuals, companies, organisations and universities across Malaysia and Europe.

**1999 - 2002**\
Worked as a Web Designer and Multimedia Designer/Developer for various local and multinational companies from Penang to Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia. Also worked as a Web Application Developer in Kuala Lumpur from June 2015 to August 2016.
